Book Recommendations

Books have been my constant companions and most reliable teachers. The collection below represents works that have shaped my thinking, challenged my assumptions, and offered new perspectives.

For each recommendation, I've written a personal essay exploring the key ideas and sharing how they've influenced me. If something catches your interest, I hope my thoughts provide a useful entry point to these transformative works.

by David Goggins

This book sparked a fire in me during a dark time, and helped me take the first steps on my way out of hell.
